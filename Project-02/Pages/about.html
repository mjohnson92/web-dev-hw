Best Easy Card Trick

Introduction

The website was created to help people learn a simple card trick. It doesn't matter if you have little to no experience, it is easy enough for anyone to learn. I chose this topic because a couple of nights before the website was first assigned, I had watched the film called The Presitge by Christopher Nolan. After being mesmerized by how incredible the film was, I decided that magic is what I would try to explore as something that could be taught.

Learning

I hope that this instructable website has enough information to help you learn something new. The process of creating this page has been fun and a great learning experience. My goal is to not forget too much by the time we start our next project! There is quite a bit of information to take in but I think we have a lot of great resources to stay confident with html.
