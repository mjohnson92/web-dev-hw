How to Perform the World's Best Easy Card Trick

Today young magicians, I will teach you a new impressive trick.
It's quite easy, and your audience will surely be surprised by its mystery.
Below you will find everything you need to learn.

The B.E.C.T. is a trick where the magician picks a shuffled card out of the deck
that the audience has chosen out of the 3 options given at the start.
Also, the magician will make the card rise out of the deck at the end for an even better surprise.

Things Required

  1. At least one witness
  2. Lighting in your favor
  3. Quick Hands
  4. Practice
  5. The magician's spirit!
  6. A deck of cards
    • Poker Size (3.5" x 2.25")
    • Bridge Size (3.5" x 2.5")

Directions

  1. Show the audience it's a normal deck of cards
    • Show with your hands that there is nothing special about the deck
    • After showing, prepare your hands for the trick
  2. Display 3 cards from the back of the deck, Hiding a 4th at the back
    • To start, pull the back card from the deck down an inch but still make sure its hidden
    • Next, pull the 3 next cards from the back of the deck upwards (leaving them in the deck) and shift them so they can be identified
    • Ask the audience to pick 1 of the 3 cards you've pulled outward
      1. You do not need to know the card
      2. You only need to remember its position
  3. Square the cards and count in as they are faced down
    • Knowing the position of the card (1,2,3), count in to the position while disposing the cards into the deck
    • Remember, there was an extra card in the back so when you finish counting in and shuffing the cards, the card they chose will be on top.
    • The audience will think the card they chose will be shuffled in the deck
  4. Face the cards back to the audience and tap a finger on the deck 3 times
    • Tap your pointer finger on the deck 3 times
    • On the 3rd slowly raise the back card with your pinky and the pointer finger

Now that you know this new trick, you can go out in public with confidence as amateur magician.
