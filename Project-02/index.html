How to Perform the World's Best Easy Card Trick

Today young magicians, we will teach you a new impressive trick.
It's quite easy, and your audience will surely be surprised by its mystery.
All you will need for this trick is a deck of cards and the will to learn.

The B.E.C.T. is a trick where the magician picks a shuffled card out of the deck that the audience has chosen out of the 3 options given at the start. also makes the card rise out of the deck at the end for an even better surprise.
