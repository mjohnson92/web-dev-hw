West Entropic

West Entropic is an online journal that shares a selection of audio and visual artwork chosen by the author of West Entropic. W.E. is a project meant to share a selection of valuable work of talented original artists. Its end goal is meant to inspire others.


The Author

Creator and founder, Michael Johnson, is an artist who wants to inspire others and himself. His goal was to create a space where he could pick favorites and honor them by writing about them.


Michael also creates music of his own!
