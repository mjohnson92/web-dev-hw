ABOUT

West Entropic is an online journal that shares a selection of underground electronic music chosen by the author of West Entropic. W.E. is a creative hub meant to share valuable work of talented original artists. It is meant to inspire others.


The Author

Creator and founder, Fox Mulder, is an artist who wants to inspire others and himself. His goal was to create a space where he could pick favorites and honor them by writing about them.


In Mulder's spare time he enjoys following UFO's in the night and hanging with his buddy, Scully.
